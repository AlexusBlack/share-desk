ShareDesk is a fast and convinient way to start sharing your screen to another person or many. This can be useful if you are experiencing some troubles and only your friend can help and advise. In compare to other solutions you don'y need to install anything or search for your IP address, just press "Start" button.

Full instruction:
1. Download and launch ShareDesk
2. Press "Start" and send appeared link to your friend.
